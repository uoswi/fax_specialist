## The Challenge

Memorial Health Network operates 12 hospitals and over 200 clinics across three states. Their fax infrastructure had evolved organically over 20 years:

- **5 different fax systems** across facilities
- **No centralized logging** for HIPAA compliance
- **Manual processes** for routing incoming faxes to the right department
- **Frequent failures** causing delayed patient referrals

When an OCR audit flagged their fax infrastructure as a compliance risk, leadership knew they needed to act fast.

## Our Approach

We designed a phased migration that prioritized compliance while minimizing disruption to patient care:

### Phase 1: Assessment & Planning (4 weeks)
- Documented all existing fax workflows across 12 hospitals
- Mapped integration points with Epic EHR
- Identified high-risk PHI transmission patterns
- Developed facility-by-facility migration timeline

### Phase 2: Infrastructure Setup (2 weeks)
- Deployed etherFAX cloud infrastructure
- Configured Epic integration modules
- Set up centralized audit logging and reporting
- Established role-based access controls

### Phase 3: Phased Rollout (12 weeks)
- Migrated one hospital per week
- Maintained parallel systems during transition
- Trained staff at each facility
- Validated compliance at each phase

### Phase 4: Optimization (4 weeks)
- Fine-tuned routing rules based on real-world usage
- Implemented automated referral workflows
- Created compliance dashboards for leadership

## The Results

The project was completed on time and under budget:

- **Zero PHI incidents** during the entire 22-week project
- **40% reduction** in referral processing times
- **Single compliance dashboard** replacing 5 separate reporting systems
- **85% reduction** in manual fax handling
- **99.9% system uptime** since go-live

## Key Takeaways

1. **Direct EHR integration is essential**: The Epic integration eliminated manual steps and reduced error rates
2. **Phased rollouts reduce risk**: One facility at a time allowed us to learn and adjust
3. **Compliance by design**: Building audit trails into the system from day one made reporting effortless
